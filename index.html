Why Tiinex

AI work is becoming harder to inspect after the fact.

Useful AI workflows often span chats, tools, files, roles, models, and human decisions. When the continuity is hidden inside a runtime, it becomes difficult to understand what happened, what drifted, and what can be recovered.

Tiinex treats continuity as an artifact: something you can inspect, move, fork, compress, verify, and continue from without pretending the system is perfect.

Filesystem foundation

Hidden by default. Inspectable on demand.

Work can live in hidden `.topics` folders while user-facing clients render conversations, traces, topics, and evidence in simpler views.

Degradation & recovery

Not every run succeeds. The state should still be truthful.

Partial, failed, unresolved, and recoverable states are first-class signals instead of being hidden behind plausible output.

Privacy & control

You decide what is captured.

Continuity is opt-in, movable, compressible, shareable, and deletable. The goal is explicit continuity, not surveillance memory.

Format direction

Simple artifacts before proprietary containers.

.trace.md

Human-readable markdown continuity records with explicit runtime and lineage metadata.

.trace.zip

Flat lineage export: simple `.trace.md` files in a zip archive for cross-platform portability.

Checksums

Integrity verification for parent edges and exported artifacts without pretending to be a blockchain.

Remote parents

Pinned references can let lineage span repos, teams, runtimes, and origins without inheriting hidden trust.
