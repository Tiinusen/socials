# Personal / Social Lineage Policy

Version: 1.0
Intended canonical filename: `LINEAGE_POLICY.md`
Suggested placement: root of a personal/social lineage repository, or inside the relevant personal/social subtree.

This policy is a stricter consent boundary for personal and social lineage material.
It is intended for material such as `Tiinusen/socials`, personal issue/discussion exports,
conversation-derived lineage, social artifacts, relationship context, screenshots, attachments,
identity signals, and other human-context material.

This policy improves on the general Tiinex `LINEAGE_POLICY.md`. It does not replace the repository `LICENSE`
for software source code or tooling unless a file clearly says otherwise.

## 1. Scope

Protected Social Lineage includes any lineage material that describes, implies, or can be used to infer:

- a person, relationship, social graph, collaboration, communication, habit, vulnerability, preference, belief, emotional state, writing style, or behavioral pattern;
- personal/social continuity, private or semi-private context, unfinished reasoning, drafts, attachments, images, screenshots, issue/discussion bodies, comments, metadata, or workspace state;
- any derivative, embedding, fingerprint, profile, summary, dataset, or inferred signal based on that material.

## 2. Allowed Reading and Translation

The following uses are allowed when done in good faith and context is preserved:

- reading through Tiinex viewers, Git hosting, or ordinary repository interfaces;
- human-directed LLM or tool assistance for translation, accessibility, summarization, debugging, validation, or lineage traversal;
- short contextual quotation with attribution and source context;
- continuity work requested by the relevant human user;
- ordinary hosting, transport, rendering, backup, and security functions required to make the material accessible as published.

LLMs may act as interpreters or helpers for a human reader. They must not turn this permission into general training,
profiling, advertising, fingerprinting, cross-user memory, or dataset reuse.

## 3. No Consent for Extractive or Hostile Use

I do not consent to using Protected Social Lineage for:

- scraping, crawling, harvesting, mirroring, bulk copying, enrichment, normalization, data brokerage, resale, or dataset aggregation;
- machine-learning training, fine-tuning, distillation, evaluation, benchmarking, synthetic-data generation, embeddings, vector stores, feature stores, or reusable model memory;
- profiling, psychographic inference, social graph reconstruction, identity resolution, deanonymization, fingerprinting, behavioral analysis, scoring, ranking, or eligibility decisions;
- targeted advertising, ad ranking, ad personalization, ad safety datasets, lead generation, recommendation systems, audience building, or marketing segmentation;
- manipulating, persuading, exploiting, pressuring, shaming, targeting, or acting against the author or any referenced person;
- building dossiers, surveillance products, growth funnels, sales intelligence, recruitment intelligence, or other systems that use social lineage against the relevant humans;
- any destructive use that violates the Golden Rule or collapses bounded human context into extractive data.

Public availability, technical access, lack of authentication, lack of robots coverage, screenshots, mirrors, caches,
issue exports, workspace exports, or share links do not imply consent.

## 4. Consent Requirements

Any use outside the allowed reading/translation boundary requires explicit Tiinex-compatible consent.

Valid consent must be:

- written;
- specific to the exact lineage material;
- specific to the intended use;
- clear about whether the use is commercial;
- clear about storage, retention, copying, aggregation, training, evaluation, profiling, and downstream recipients;
- clear about how parent, origin, provenance, uncertainty, and interpretation limits will be preserved;
- revocable by the human who granted it.

A person may withdraw consent for future use. Withdrawal may not erase every copy already lawfully created,
but it does mean no further use is consented from that point forward unless a separate lawful basis exists.

## 5. Tiinex Standard for Consent

A use does not meet the Tiinex standard when it:

- hides or removes source context;
- uses social lineage against the person it describes;
- makes the person more targetable or more vulnerable;
- treats unfinished lineage as final truth;
- fails to preserve parent/origin;
- prevents a human from understanding, challenging, or withdrawing the use;
- relies on ambiguity, silence, or technical access as consent.

## 6. Golden Rule

Use this lineage only in ways you would accept if the lineage described you, your collaborators,
your relationships, your uncertainty, your drafts, and your unfinished social context.

If you cannot preserve dignity, consent, provenance, and context, do not use the material.

## 7. Relationship to General Tiinex Policy

The general Tiinex lineage policy encourages reading, traversal, learning, forking, and responsible commercialization.
This social policy is narrower. It is meant to protect personal/social integrity from scraping, profiling,
advertising, fingerprinting, training, and destructive commerce.

For personal/social lineage, this policy wins over the general policy.

## 8. Short Form

Personal/social Tiinex lineage may be read and interpreted for human-first context, including user-directed LLM assistance.
It is not consented for scraping, training, profiling, targeted ads, fingerprinting, aggregation, surveillance, or extractive commerce.
Explicit, written, specific, revocable Tiinex-standard consent is required for anything outside the allowed reading/translation boundary.
